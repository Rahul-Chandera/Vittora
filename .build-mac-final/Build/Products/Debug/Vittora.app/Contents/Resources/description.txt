Vittora is a private personal finance app built for people who want clarity without compromise.

Track spending, income, budgets, recurring payments, savings goals, tax estimates, receipts, and reports across iPhone, iPad, and Mac. Your data stays under your control with local-first storage and iCloud sync through your own Apple account.

Why people choose Vittora:
- Private by design with no analytics SDKs or ad trackers
- Fast transaction entry with clean categories and account support
- Budgets, recurring rules, payees, debt tracking, and savings goals
- Receipt scanning, document attachments, and searchable history
- Tax estimates for India and the United States with comparison tools
- Works across Apple devices with a native SwiftUI experience

Vittora is designed to feel calm, fast, and trustworthy so you can focus on your money decisions instead of wrestling with your tools.
