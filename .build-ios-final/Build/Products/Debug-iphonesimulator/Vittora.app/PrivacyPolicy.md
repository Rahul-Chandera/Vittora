# Vittora Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 16, 2026

## Overview

Vittora is designed to help you manage personal finances while keeping your information private. We do not sell your data, we do not include third-party analytics SDKs, and we do not run advertising profiles on your financial activity.

## What data Vittora stores

Vittora may store the following information that you enter or attach:

- Accounts, balances, transactions, categories, budgets, recurring rules, savings goals, payees, and debt records
- Receipt images, PDFs, and OCR-derived document details
- Tax profiles and tax estimate inputs
- App settings such as currency, appearance, lock preferences, and onboarding completion

## Where your data lives

Your data is stored locally on your device and may sync through Apple's iCloud services when sync is enabled and available for your Apple account. Vittora is built around your private iCloud data and does not rely on third-party backend storage for your financial records.

## How your data is used

Your data is used only to provide app functionality such as:

- Displaying dashboards, reports, and budgets
- Syncing across your devices using iCloud
- Importing, scanning, and attaching receipts or documents
- Calculating summaries, recurring items, savings progress, and tax estimates

## What Vittora does not do

Vittora does not:

- Sell personal information
- Share financial data with advertisers
- Include third-party analytics or marketing trackers
- Use your financial records to build advertising profiles

## Permissions

Depending on the features you use, Vittora may request access to:

- Camera: to scan receipts and capture documents
- Photos: to attach receipt images to transactions
- Contacts: to help associate payees or debt records with people and businesses

You can deny these permissions and continue using other areas of the app.

## Security

Vittora is designed with privacy-conscious defaults, including local-first storage, Apple platform security features, and app-level protections where available. No system can guarantee absolute security, but we aim to minimize unnecessary data exposure and external sharing.

## Tax information disclaimer

Tax calculations, comparisons, and exported tax reports are estimates only. Vittora does not provide legal, tax, or financial advice. You should consult a qualified professional before acting on tax information.

## Changes

This policy may be updated as Vittora evolves. Material updates should be reflected by revising the date at the top of this document.

## Contact

For privacy-related questions, contact the Vittora support channel associated with your distribution or release build.
